How to Use This Workbook

This workbook is designed to be flexible. Kids can work through the activities at their own pace, and the same content adapts to different schedules. Pick the pacing that fits YOUR situation.

Three Pacing Options

1. 1-Week Intensive

Pace: 2–3 activities per day
Timeline: Complete in 5–7 days
Best for: Summer camps, spring break, gifted kids who love to binge, or a focused CS week

2. 4-Week Unit

Pace: 3 activities per week
Timeline: Complete in ~4 weeks
Best for: Homeschool weekly rhythm, structured after-school time, or a monthly enrichment plan

3. 12-Week Light Schedule

Pace: 1 activity per week
Timeline: Complete over a full semester
Best for: Elementary CS class, gentle weekly drip, or when you want deep discussion time on each concept

Materials Needed

  • A pencil (or crayons for younger kids)
  • Scissors and glue (only for cut-and-paste bonus activities)
  • 5–10 small everyday objects (only for hands-on bonuses like sorting)
  • A curious mind — that's it!

Tips for Success

  • 1Do the BASIC version first, then try the CHALLENGE version if ready
  • 2Read the "How It Works" sections carefully — they explain the rules for each new concept
  • 3The bonus activities are the MOST fun — don't skip them! Many are hands-on or social
  • 4It's OK to make mistakes. Debugging is a real CS skill — finding what went wrong is half the learning
